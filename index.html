Philosophical Framework

I am a visionary artist and philosopher committed to crafting a new vocabulary for the post-modern world. My work integrates metaphysical principles, esoteric practices, and practical self-improvement to redefine human potential through art, spirituality, and philosophy.

Core Philosophies:

  • The Meta-Inquisitive Method: A self-healing process through introspection and paradox.
  • Self-Creation: Viewing art as a process of self-defining conditions to achieve dreams.
  • Transcending Linear Thought: Balancing opposites to navigate complex human experiences.
  • Practical Mysticism: Utilizing Kabbalah, Tarot, and astrology for transformation.
  • Integration of Myth: Exploring mythopoetic expression for meaningful action.

Projects and Ventures

My work spans mystical and esoteric systems, public speaking, writing, and education.

Highlights:

  • Developing structured esoteric systems, including rituals and memory palaces.
  • Writing articles and speeches on ego transcendence and integrating self-discipline with artistry.
  • Facilitating workshops on foundational texts like Meditation and Kabbalah and Thus Spoke Zarathustra.
  • Providing Tarot readings and philosophical consultations.
  • Authoring a comprehensive guidebook on metaphysics and societal qualities.
